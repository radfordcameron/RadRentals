Policies

Please take a moment to read through our policies

Reservations

We require customers to make a reservation through our website or on the phone. Items are reserved with a $30 deposit, that will go towards the cost of your rental. If you have not made a payment items are first come first serve. Our prices do not include delivery. The rental is from 10:00am - 9:00am the following day (23 hours). If you rent a bounce house on Saturday it will be due the back the following Monday at 9:00am. Failure to return the rental by 9:00am will result in a $60 late fee plus $100 for every additional day it is not returned. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone, for any reason. All renters must be at least 18 years of age. A valid driver's licence is required to pickup. Please contact us if you have any questions or special requests.

Security Deposit

A security deposit will be automatically collected the day of the rental. The deposit is refunded when the product is returned on time, clean, dry and in working order. If rental is returned damaged, wet, dirty or late it will result in forfiture of your security deposit. If repair or replacement costs exceeds the security deposit you will be responsible for the full costs of repair or replacement. If you fail to return the rented items you will be charged the full replacement cost.

Cancellation Policy

Items are only reserved by scheduling on our website or by phone. If you decide to cancel your reservation 100% of the deposit can be used towards a future rental. A minimum notice of 24 hours before reservation start time (10:00am the day prior) is required, cancellation within 24 hours will result in forfeiture of reservation with no refund or voucher issued. In case of inclement weather the 24 hour policy will be waived and you will recive a voucher for 100% of amount paid towards a future rental. We recommend that you arrange a alternative indoor location in case of inclement weather. Radford Rentals will determine what classifies as inclement weather.

Cleaning

All rented items must be returned clean and dry. We reseve the right to charge fees associated with repair, cleaning, or replacement of rented items. Returning items wet, damp, dirty or late will result in forfeiture of security deposit.

Summary

We are happy that you are considering us for your rental. We strive to ensure that every customer is treated fairly. We have searched the market to ensure that we have the lowest prices on rentals and the most liberal policies to ensure your satisfaction.
