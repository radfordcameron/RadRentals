Policies

Please take a moment to read through our policies

Reservations

We require customers to make a reservation through our website or on the phone. Items are reserved with a payment, if you have not made a payment items are first come first serve. A $100 security deposit will be automatically collected the day of the rental. The deposit is refunded when the product is returned clean, dry and in working order. Our prices do not include delivery. Pickup time is after 8:00 am the day of your rental. Please contact us if you have any questions or special requests .

Cancellation Policy

Items are only reserved by scheduling on our website, if a reservation has not been made the items will be first come first serve. We have a liberal cancellation policy, you have 14 days before the date of your rental to reschedule. If you would like to cancel, or reschedule within 14 days of your rental please contact us as soon as possible. If we are able to rent the items to someone else you will not be charged for the rental. If we are unable to rent the item to another customer 100% of the rental price can be used towards any future rental. In case of inclement weather we will allow you to reschedule.

Cleaning

All rented items must be returned clean and dry. We reseve the right to charge fees associated with repair, cleaning, or replacement of rented items. Returning items wet or dirty will result in forfeiture of security deposit.

Summary

We are happy that you are considering us for your rental. We strive to ensure that every customer is treated fairly. We have searched the market to ensure that we have the lowest prices on rentals and the most liberal policies to ensure your satisfaction.
